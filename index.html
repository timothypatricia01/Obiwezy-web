Risk Mitigation

Fraud & Counterfeits

Risk: Bad actors, fake devices

Solution: Multi-layer KYC, IMEI blacklist checking, user trust scores, 2% insurance fund

Logistics Failures

Risk: Damaged/lost devices in transit

Solution: Mandatory pre-pickup photos, package insurance, courier accountability, immediate credit for losses

Escrow Disputes

Risk: Condition mismatch claims

Solution: Detailed condition checklist, 48-hour inspection window, neutral arbitration team, video unboxing incentives

Low Initial Liquidity

Risk: Not enough swap matches

Solution: Obiwezy buys inventory, "guaranteed swap" program, AI suggests similar devices, partner with refurbishers
